This is CUSD !!!

Founded in 2009, Cornell University Sustainable Design (CUSD) is a student run, interdisciplinary project team with a bias towards impact. We bring together students from across all seven colleges of Cornell to work alongside renowned faculty and knowledgeable professionals in our mission to create a network of proactively sustainable communities. With multiple innovative projects from the past, present, and future, we continue to produce creative results that make a huge impact in the world around us. The success of our team is also a result of support from sponsors and partners that are dedicated to helping us overcome our obstacles.
