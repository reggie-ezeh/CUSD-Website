Pilot Program

Didn't get a chance to apply to CUSD before the Fall 2018 Recruitment deadline?

The CUSD Freshman Pilot Program is an intensive one-semester program that will immerse highly motivated first-year students in the world of CUSD. Once accepted, students will engage in our new member education series to learn background knowledge, leadership ability, and technical skills necessary to contribute to the teams of their choice. The program will transition its recruits to full team members by the end of the fall semester.

CUSD invites motivated students from all majors to come to one of our two information sessions to learn more about what we do and how to get involved.
